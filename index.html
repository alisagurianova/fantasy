Minsky's Quest

A pixel-art platform adventure across 18 screens and 7 biomes. Inspired by classic Lode Runner.

Goal
Collect all 4 ancient seals hidden in chests across the world and return them to the altar in the Final Chamber.

Seals to find
🗺 Ancient Map — Deep Forest
🔑 Desert Key — Buried Temple
💎 Mountain Gem — Eagle Peak
🏛 Temple Seal — Ruins Interior

Tips
• Talk to the blacksmith in the first town to get your sword.
• Rest at inns to fully restore HP for free.
• Enemies drop gold — use it to buy potions at shops.
• Desert Boots (Oasis Town shop) slow down enemies.
• Gravity applies — only ladders and ropes let you go up.
